How it works

From enquiry to accepted quote — without the paperwork

Coglass handles every step of the quoting process. Here's exactly how it works.

1
Enquiry comes in
Customers submit enquiries via the embedded web form on your website, or your team creates an order directly in Coglass. Customer name, address, contact details and job description are all captured.
2
Add glass specifications
Add each pane to the quote: dimensions (width × height in mm), quantity, product type (cut size, DGU, toughened, laminated), and any operations like polished edges, cutouts or Georgian bars.
3
Price is calculated automatically
Coglass calculates the price based on square metre rates or unit pricing from your product catalogue. Labour, installation charges and energy allowances are added automatically or adjusted manually.
4
Send the PDF quote
Click Send. Coglass generates a branded PDF quote and emails it to your customer. A unique public link lets them view the quote online without needing a login — on any device.
5
Customer accepts online
The customer clicks Accept on their quote page. Coglass instantly notifies your team, converts the quote to a live order, and assigns it an order number — no calls, no back and forth.
6
Order is ready to process
The accepted order lands in your Orders screen with all glass specifications intact, ready for the production planning board, supplier ordering, and team scheduling.
