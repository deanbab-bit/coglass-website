How it works

From customer order to job in Coglass — automatically

The webshop handles the order, the payment and the confirmation. Your team just sees a new order waiting to be processed.

1
Customer visits your shop
Your trade customers visit your branded glass shop — either at its own URL or embedded directly on your existing website via a copy-paste iframe. They browse your enabled products by category.
2
Configure the glass
For each product, they enter dimensions in millimetres and configure options — Georgian bars (positions, colour, width), lead design (diamond or square, cell size, colour), cutouts (circles, rectangles, letterboxes, notches) with live price updating.
3
Checkout and pay
Customers enter their details and choose how to pay: submit as an enquiry, pay by card via Stripe, or pay via PayPal. Trade customers who are logged in automatically get their trade discount applied at checkout.
4
Order lands in Coglass
The moment the order is placed, it appears in your Coglass admin panel as a webshop enquiry — with full customer details, all glass specifications, dimensions and payment status. No re-entering data, no calls to take the order.
5
Convert to a live order
Review the enquiry and convert it to a full Coglass order with one click. All glass lines, dimensions and operations carry across — the job goes straight into production planning and supplier ordering.
6
Customer tracks their order
Logged-in customers can view their order history and status in the customer portal — new, awaiting payment, paid, converted. Confirmation emails are sent automatically when orders are placed and updated.
