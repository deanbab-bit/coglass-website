How it works

Every customer conversation, linked to the job

No more hunting through WhatsApp to find what was said about a job. Every message is stored in Coglass against the order it belongs to.

1
Connect your channels
Connect your WhatsApp Business account via Meta Cloud API, your email via Gmail or Outlook, and SMS via your SMS provider. One-time setup — Coglass handles the rest.
2
Message from the order
Open any order in Coglass and send a WhatsApp message, SMS or email directly from the order screen. The customer's contact details are pre-filled — no copying numbers between apps.
3
Replies come in automatically
When a customer replies via WhatsApp, the message appears in Coglass against the same order. Your team sees the full conversation history without leaving the job screen.
4
Set up automated notifications
Configure automation rules that trigger SMS or email messages automatically when job status changes — glass ready, fitter on the way, job complete. Your customers stay informed without anyone lifting a finger.
5
Use message templates
Pre-written templates for common messages — quote sent, glass ready, payment reminder, appointment reminder. Variables like {customerName} and {orderNumber} fill in automatically from the job.
6
Full message history
Every message ever sent or received on any channel is stored in the order timeline. New team members can pick up any job and immediately see the full customer communication history.
