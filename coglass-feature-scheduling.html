How it works

Your whole team's week, planned in minutes

The Coglass planning board gives managers a full view of who is doing what, when — and pushes the schedule straight to everyone's phones.

1
Open the planning board
The scheduling view shows your full team in rows and upcoming dates in columns. At a glance you can see who's free, who's booked, and which days have gaps — for the week or the next two weeks.
2
Assign jobs to team members
Drag an order onto a team member's slot for a specific date. Set the visit type (survey, fitting, delivery), start time and duration. The assignment appears in that person's mobile app immediately — no call needed.
3
Allocate vehicles
Assign a van to each team member for each day from the same board. Field staff select their vehicle in the mobile app when they start their day — vehicle and driver are tracked together.
4
Block absences
Record holidays, sick days and training in the absence calendar. Absent team members show as blocked on the planning board — you can't accidentally book them in on a day they're off.
5
Team see their schedule on mobile
Every assignment syncs to the field team's mobile app in real time. They open the app and see exactly what they're doing today — customer, address, visit type and time — without any calls from the office.
6
Manage your people
The employees section tracks staff details, roles, certifications, and mobile app access. Monitor seat usage, manage who has access to which parts of the system, and view each person's upcoming schedule at a glance.
