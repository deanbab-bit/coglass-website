How it works

Close the job out properly, on site

Coglass turns a finished fitting into clean proof — and tells the office exactly what’s outstanding.

1
Open the fitting visit
The fitter opens the booked visit in the app — the full glass list, drawings and access notes from the survey are already there.
2
Tick off each pane
Mark every item Fitted, Partial or Not-fitted. “Mark all as fitted” clears a clean job in one tap.
3
Log the reason
Not-fitted or partial? Pick a reason — wrong size, broken, scratched — and Coglass flags it for re-order back at the office.
4
Add photos & snags
Attach installation photos and snag notes against each item so the office sees exactly what happened.
5
Capture the signature
The customer signs on the phone to confirm completion — stored against the job with GPS and time.
6
Auto-complete & invoice
When every pane is fitted, the job moves to completed and a draft invoice is created automatically for the office to send.
