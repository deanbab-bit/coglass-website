How it works

One place for the whole job lifecycle

Coglass moves a job through every stage — and keeps everyone looking at the same record.

1
Capture the enquiry
Phone, email, WhatsApp or your website form — the customer, site address, contacts and job description are captured into a new order.
2
Quote or survey
Send a quote or book a survey straight from the order. Everything stays attached to the same job.
3
Track the status
Watch the job move through new request → survey → quote → on order → glass ready → fitting → completed, with the right label for collection, delivery or glazing.
4
Keep notes & photos
Internal notes, site photos and documents live on the order so the whole team has the full picture — office and field.
5
Never-reused numbers
Every order gets a unique, monotonic number that is never re-issued — even after a job is deleted — so your records stay clean.
6
Through to invoice
When the job completes, raise the invoice, take payment and push to Xero — all from the same order.
