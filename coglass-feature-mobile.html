How it works

Your team's full working day — in one app

From arriving on site to collecting the customer's signature, the Coglass app handles every step.

1
See today's jobs
Field staff open the app and see their assigned jobs for the day — customer name, address, visit type (survey, fitting, delivery) and time slot. All assigned by the office via the planning board.
2
Check the job details
Tap any job to see the full order: customer contact, site address, access notes, special instructions, previous survey photos and glass specifications. Everything they need before ringing the doorbell.
3
Capture the survey
Start a survey visit, measure each pane, add drawings and photos. All measurements are captured in millimetres with location labels, shape, operations and notes — synced to the office when back in signal.
4
Record the fitting
On a fitting visit, work through the completion checklist. Mark items as fitted, record snag notes and photos, capture GPS proof of location, and note any access issues or follow-up work needed.
5
Collect signature
Once work is complete, the customer signs directly on the screen. The signed record is stored against the job — time-stamped, GPS-tagged and available in the office immediately.
6
Works offline
No signal on site? The app keeps working. All data is captured and queued locally. The moment signal returns, everything syncs automatically — photos, measurements, signatures, all of it.
