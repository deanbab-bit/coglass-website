▢ Coglass

Support

Coglass is field-service software for glazing companies. The app is used by staff at firms that subscribe to Coglass — you'll sign in with an account from your company.

Get in touch

The fastest way to reach us is email — we usually reply within one working day.

support@coglass.co.uk

For anything about your jobs, customers or company data, your company's Coglass administrator can help quickest — they manage your account and settings.

Signing in

On the sign-in screen you'll need three things, all provided by your company:

  • Company address — e.g. yourcompany.coglass.app
  • Username
  • Password

Coglass doesn't offer public sign-up — accounts are created by each company for its own staff.

Common questions

I don't have a login

Ask your manager or your company's Coglass administrator to set you up. If your company isn't on Coglass yet, see coglass.co.uk.

I've forgotten my password

Your company administrator can reset it for you. If you're the administrator, email support@coglass.co.uk.

What's my company address?

It's your company's Coglass web address (e.g. yourcompany.coglass.app). Your manager or administrator will have it.

Why does the app ask for my location?

For field staff, location powers your day on the job — navigation to sites and your arrive/depart times. It's only used while you're using the app, and you can turn it off in your device settings (some on-site features won't work without it).

The app won't sync / I'm offline

Coglass works offline and syncs automatically when you're back in signal. If something looks out of date, pull to refresh or re-open the screen once you have a connection.

Your account & data

When you use Coglass through your employer, your company is the controller of the business and customer data, and Coglass processes it on their behalf.

Deleting your account or data

To delete your account or personal data, contact your company administrator, or email support@coglass.co.uk and we'll action it (or pass it to your company where they control the data). See our Privacy Policy for full details.
