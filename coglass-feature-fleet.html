How it works

Fleet management without a separate system

Everything you need to keep your vehicles compliant and your team mobile — built into the same system you use for jobs.

1
Add your vehicles
Add each van with its name (e.g. "Van 1"), registration plate, make, model and MOT due date. Coglass uses this to track compliance and send you reminders before the MOT expires.
2
Upload certificates
Store MOT certificates, insurance documents and any other vehicle paperwork directly in Coglass against the vehicle record. No more hunting through email or filing cabinets when something needs checking.
3
Assign vehicles daily
From the planning board, assign a van to each team member for each day. Field staff see their assigned vehicle in the mobile app and confirm it when starting their day.
4
Get MOT reminders
Coglass alerts you when a vehicle's MOT is approaching — configurable days in advance. No vehicle goes overdue because it was forgotten among the noise of running a busy glazing business.
5
Track assignment history
Every vehicle assignment is logged — who drove which vehicle on which date. Full history available for insurance queries, incident investigations or simply checking what's been used most.
6
DVLA lookup
Look up vehicle information directly from DVLA when adding a new vehicle. Registration plate to full vehicle details in one click — make, model, colour and MOT history pre-filled automatically.
