How it works

From counter to collection — the merchant's day

Coglass runs the whole flow a glass merchant works through, on one system.

1
Take the order
Counter, phone, email — or a branded webshop where trade customers reorder cut-to-size on their own agreed prices. Quick Go builds a walk-in quote or order in seconds.
2
Price it like glass
Per-m² and per-unit pricing with trade price-lists, quantity breaks and per-customer accounts. No retyping rate cards — the right price applies automatically.
3
Spec every pane
Full IGU make-ups (DGU/TGU, cavity, spacer, gas), edges, shapes and operations — with the spacer/cavity captured per line, never baked into the product.
4
Onto the floor
The production board tracks every order through the shop with barcode scan from cut to ready. Make each operation in-house or send it out as outwork — Coglass raises the processing order automatically.
5
Collect or deliver
Collection orders with a signature at the counter, or route-planned delivery runs for cut glass — because not everything gets fitted.
6
Get paid
Deposits, proformas, UK VAT and account invoicing, pushed to Xero, with overdue chasing built in.
