How it works

From unit order to loaded rack

Coglass runs the commercial and production side of a sealed unit business, on one system.

1
Take the unit order
Trade customers order by make-up and size — at the counter, by email, or a branded webshop where they reorder on their agreed prices. Quick Go builds a unit order in seconds.
2
Spec the make-up
Full DGU/TGU build: the glass for each leaf (clear, toughened, laminated, low-E, obscure), cavity width, spacer bar and gas — captured per line, never baked into the product, so you avoid a 12/16/20mm variant of every unit.
3
Price it on trade terms
Per-unit and per-m² pricing with trade price-lists, quantity breaks and per-customer accounts. The right price applies automatically — no retyping rate cards.
4
Into production
Every unit order lands on the production board and moves from cut to ready, with barcode labels per piece. Cut and process in-house, or buy in toughened / laminated leaves as outwork.
5
Onto the rack & out
Collection with a signature at the counter, or route-planned delivery runs for finished units — units go out on stillages, not vans full of fitters.
6
Get paid
Deposits, proformas, UK VAT and account invoicing, synced to Xero, with overdue chasing built in.
