How it works

Survey to glass order — without the guesswork

The Coglass survey tool captures everything your office needs to order the right glass, first time.

1
Open the job on your phone
Your surveyor opens the assigned job in the Coglass mobile app. Job address, access notes and survey instructions from the office are all visible before they arrive on site.
2
Start the survey visit
Tap Start Survey. Coglass records the time, date and who is on site. The surveyor can record how many operatives are present, access requirements (ladders, scaffolding, powered access), and any special notes.
3
Add measurements
For each pane, the surveyor enters a location label (e.g. "Front bedroom left"), width and height in millimetres, and quantity. All dimensions are stored exactly as entered — no unit conversion, no rounding.
4
Add drawings and operations
Use the drawing tool to record glass shape (rectangle, arch, circle, oval, gable, trapezoid), Georgian bar positions, lead design patterns, cutouts, holes, notches, and RAL paint codes — all linked to that measurement.
5
Take photos
Attach photos to each individual measurement and to the overall survey. Photos are stored against the job and viewable in the office immediately — useful for checking the frame, damage, or unusual details.
6
Import to order
Once the survey is finished, office staff click Import to Workbench. Each measurement becomes a product line on the order — dimensions, shape, operations and all — ready for pricing and supplier ordering.
