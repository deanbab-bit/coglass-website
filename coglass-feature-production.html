How it works

From order placed to glass ready — fully tracked

The production board keeps your workshop and your office in sync at all times.

1
Items appear on the board
When an order is placed, every glass line automatically appears on the production board under To Plan. Expected ready dates are calculated from your supplier lead times so your team knows what to prioritise immediately.
2
Move items through the workflow
Drag cards across the board as work progresses: To Plan → Waiting Supplier → In Production → Ready → Made. Every status change is timestamped and logged against the order.
3
Flag urgent jobs
Mark any item as Rush with a reason. Rush cards are highlighted in red and sorted to the top of every column. Your team can see at a glance what needs to be prioritised today.
4
Print barcode labels
Generate barcode labels directly from the production board. Each label shows the order number, customer, pane description and dimensions. Stick them to the glass in the workshop — scan to confirm receipt or completion.
5
Get overdue alerts
If an item's expected ready date passes without being moved to Ready, Coglass automatically alerts the relevant team member. Nothing slips through the cracks without someone being notified.
6
Mark as made
Once glass is fitted or delivered, mark the item as Made. Full production history is logged against the order — when it was made, who moved it, and how long it spent in each stage.
